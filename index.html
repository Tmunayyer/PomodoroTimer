About The Pomodoro Timer

To help fight procrastination and to protect yourself from burnout use a Pomodoro Timer!

Studies have shown that a person is most effective when he/she works for 25 minutes and then takes a 5 minute break to decompress. Attention spans are unlikely to last much longer than 25 minutes. In working longer than 25 minutes you are putting yourself at risk to overload your short term memory and burnout!

Pomodoro Timers are also effective at combatting procrastination. If your having trouble starting a task you dont want to do then set a timer. Usually youll become engrossed in the task before the timer is up.
